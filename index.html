Bella's Bumbas

Bella’s Bumbas is a movement that helps kids with mobile disabilities. This was created by Isabella Shorr’s family after Isabella was born and diagnosed with spina bifida. Spina Bifida is a condition where your spine is on the outside of your body, which can cause paralysis of the lower limbs, and sometimes mental handicap. Isabella is currently paralyzed from knees down, but with Bella’s Bumbas, she is able to move around and have freedom. Bella’s Bumbas are wheelchairs for children with spina bifida, and it works just like a wheelchair. It also has a seat-belt and a brake. For more information you can visit their facebook page
